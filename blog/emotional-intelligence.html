7 April 2024: Emotional Intelligence.


What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence or EQ is the ability to positively perceive, understand, regulate and manage your emotions and recognize and influence the emotions around you. Research suggests that EQ is the strongest predictor of performance in the workplace with high emotional intelligence are more likely to respond under pressure, respond to colleagues with empathy and effectively resolve conflict.

The 4 Core Competencies of Emotional Intelligence

How is EQ different to IQ?

As outlined earlier in this blog post, EQ refers to your emotional abilities and social skills. Whereas IQ measures your cognitive and intellectual skills such as knowledge retention, analytical thinking and working memory.

Why is EQ important?

Emotional intelligence affects not only performance at the workplace, but it can also affect your physical health, your mental health, your relationships and your social intelligence. People who possess high EQ are more likely to live well rounded, happy and fulfilled lives.
