Margin, Border & Padding - What's the difference?


When talking about margin, padding and border we are talking about the layout and design of a webpages content.

Padding: creates space between the content and its border. Padding is transparent.

Border: is the defined border surrounding an html element. You can specify the style width and color of an elements border.
